About Us

Noah's Locker is a fashion and lifestyle brand for Men and Women of discernment and class. Our ethos is simple; There is nothing more appealing than a well-dressed person. Noah's Locker is where tradition and style meet with class , confidence, charm and timeless elegance to look effortlessly sharp in shoes and clothes that are handmade to order by expert craftsmen.

We take inspiration from AFRICAN / BRITISH fashion, creating an art that you can invest in. Focus is on handmaking all products within the Italy sourcing materials from the best suppliers all over the world.
